Glev is the voice-first meal tracker built for people living with Type 1 Diabetes. Speak your meal, and Glev parses carbs, protein and fat with AI — every entry is automatically linked to your CGM glucose reading.

What you can do with Glev:

• Log a meal in seconds — by voice, tap, or quick-entry shortcuts
• Get macros calculated automatically (carbs, protein, fat, calories)
• Pull your current glucose from FreeStyle Libre 2 (via LibreLinkUp) at log time
• Record insulin doses, exercise, cycle and symptoms in one place
• Review Time in Range, meal patterns and workout reactions on the Insights page
• Export your data as CSV — you stay in control

What Glev is NOT:

Glev is not a medical device, not an insulin calculator, and not a diabetes coach. Glev does not calculate insulin doses and does not give therapy recommendations. Therapy decisions stay between you and your endocrinologist or diabetes team. Glev is a documentation and organisation tool that helps you understand the relationship between food, activity and glucose.

Who Glev is for:

People with Type 1 Diabetes who use a CGM and want a fast, everyday way to log meals and glucose responses — without juggling three different apps every time. Currently supported: FreeStyle Libre 2 via LibreLinkUp. Dexcom and Nightscout are in progress.

Privacy:

Glucose, meal and insulin data are stored in the EU region (Supabase). Authentication runs through Supabase, AI features through Replit AI Integrations. Full details in our privacy policy: https://glev.app/legal/privacy
